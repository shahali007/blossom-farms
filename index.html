About Blossom Farms

Blossom Farms' mission is to produce food crops, fish and animal products for sale within and outside Nigeria. At full production, the farm will contribute to Nigeria's food sufficiency and enable the demonstration of good agronomic practices. Blossom Farm is also committed to the development of the local communities where it operates through the provision of decent employment and social amenities.

We employ and maintain a team of highly professional agricultural and management staff on site. We use state of the art farm equipment to maximize production. Our farm is designed in such a way that ensures interdependence between our farm product and animals which greatly reduces the cost of production of our farm produce. This also helps ensure that the animals are not exposed to a lot of chemicals. Animal wastes are used as manure in the production of crops which reduces the use of chemical fertilizers thus making our crops more organic.

What We Do ?


Farm Produce

We grow cereals, tubers, palm and fruits for market. These are produced using organic techniques to ensure that people enjoy healthy and tasty portions of their favourite staple foods. Our variety of crops makes Blossom farm an ideal place to source your foodstuffs.

Animal Husbandry

Blossom Farms work hard to supply our customers with accessible, affordable and healthy animals like chickens, snails, catfish, dairy meat, eggs. We even breed foreign dogs. We use the most innovative animal husbandry techniques to ensure the best of standards in animal husbandry.

Why Choose Us ?


Premium quality

Our well thought and crafted nutritional programs ensure that our crops and animals get their required nutrients which result in highly nutritious food products. Due to proper handling, our farm produce stays fresh for a longer time.

100% Organic

At Blossom Farms, we strive to only use natural techniques. This ensures that our farm produce and animals are free of pesticides and harmful chemicals and this is basically what you need to lead a healthy life as our products are 100% organic.
